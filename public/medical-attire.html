Medical Attire

Cherokee | Infinity

Our Medical Attire service provides high-quality, functional, and professional uniforms tailored specifically for the healthcare industry. We understand that medical professionals require attire that is not only durable and comfortable but also meets hygiene standards and safety requirements. From hospitals and clinics to laboratories and pharmacies, we supply a complete range of medical clothing that supports the day-to-day demands of healthcare workers.

SCRUBS & LAB COATS

Available in various sizes, colors, and cuts, our scrubs and lab coats are made from breathable, anti-bacterial, and easy-care fabrics suitable for long shifts and sterile environments.

CUSTOM UNIFORMS

We offer embroidery of names, logos, and department designations — ensuring your medical team maintains a unified and professional identity.

NURSING & SPECIALIST WEAR

From nursing dresses and tunics to surgical gowns and PPE for frontline responders, our range is designed for functionality, comfort, and durability.

ECO-FRIENDLY & PERFORMANCE FABRICS

Our attire uses advanced textiles including moisture-wicking, antimicrobial protection, and sustainable materials supporting greener healthcare environments.

WHY CHOOSE US

We combine quality craftsmanship, reliable sourcing, and customization capabilities to deliver attire that reflects your organization’s standards and professionalism. With our experience and commitment to service, we help healthcare providers focus on what matters most—caring for others—while we take care of what they wear.
