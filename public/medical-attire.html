Medical Attire

Cherokee | Infinity


Our Medical Attire service provides high-quality, functional, and professional uniforms tailored specifically for the healthcare industry. We understand that medical professionals require attire that is not only durable and comfortable but also meets hygiene standards, safety requirements, and presents a polished appearance. From hospitals and clinics to laboratories and pharmacies, we supply a complete range of medical clothing that supports the day-to-day demands of healthcare workers.

SCRUBS & LAB COATS

Available in various sizes, colors, and cuts, our scrubs and lab coats are made from breathable, anti-bacterial, and easy-care fabrics suitable for long shifts and sterile environments.

CUSTOM UNIFORMS

We offer embroidery of names, logos, and department designations—ensuring your medical team maintains a unified and professional identity.

NURSING & SPECIALIST WEAR
