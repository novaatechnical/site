Medical Equipment & Rehabilitation Equipment Supply

As a company registered and licensed with the Medical Device Authority (MDA) Malaysia, we supply a wide range of medical and rehabilitation equipment to meet the needs of healthcare facilities, therapy centres, and home-care providers. All products are sourced from trusted manufacturers and comply with regulatory standards to ensure safety, reliability, and performance.

WHAT WE OFFER

  • Medical Equipment
    Clinical and diagnostic devices for professional healthcare use. (Examples: patient monitors, oxygen concentrators, examination beds)
  • Rehabilitation Equipment
    Hydrotherapy Equipment – water-based therapy tools for muscle relaxation and rehabilitation.
    Physiotherapy Equipment – devices for electrotherapy, traction, exercise, and therapy treatments.
    Mobility Enhancement Equipment – wheelchairs, walkers, gait trainers, and other mobility aids.
    Other Rehabilitation Solutions – orthopaedic supports, balance and coordination tools, and therapy accessories.

WHY CHOOSE US

Registered and licensed with MDA Malaysia
Certified, high-quality, and compliant products
Customised equipment solutions based on your facility’s requirements
Competitive pricing with warranty and after-sales support

WHO WE SERVE

  • Hospitals & Clinics
  • Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation Centres
  • Elderly Care Homes
  • Home-Care Patients
  • Corporate & Occupational Health Providers
